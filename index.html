A new curriculum of project-based learning

Brave new world

It's a brave new world out there. Our children are being put in increasing more competitive situations as they move into the adult world of examinations, jobs, careers and other life choices. The landscape is quickly changing in all areas of vocation, from technology to trade occupations.

Our new curriculum faces these challenges head on. Not only do we provide the academic instruction necessary, but we also provide a more project-based learning model with real-world applications. This gives our students a more practical knowledge, with hands-on experience, to take with them as they enter the competitive workforce.

The best start in life

At Black Elk, we pride ourselves in not only giving our students a top quality education, but also giving them the societal and emotional intelligence needed to win big in their future worlds. We not only excel at subjects such as genetics, data mining, and chemistry, but we also include compulsory lessons in:

  • Emotional control
  • Judgement
  • Assertion
  • Focus and resolve
